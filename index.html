School Facilities

The KG Campus is a purpose-built building located behind the Main Campus. Classrooms are furnished with age-appropriate furniture for young children and have a warm and well-lit environment to make transition from home to school gentle and smooth. There are dedicated spaces to display work of students and open floor space for group activities.

School Bus

The school bus service ensures safe and comfortable transportation for students, with well-trained drivers and aides to assist children.

Playground

The playground is spacious and equipped with age-appropriate play structures such as swings, slides, and climbing frames, promoting physical activity, social interaction, and imaginative play.

Healthy Canteen

The healthy canteen offers a variety of nutritious snacks and meals, ensuring that children receive well-balanced food that supports their growth and energy levels throughout the day.

Positive Learning

The positive learning environment fosters creativity, curiosity, and collaboration through colorful classrooms, engaging materials, and interactive teaching methods.

Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At CT Sunshine Kindergarten, we believe in fostering a rich and diverse learning environment where children not only acquire academic knowledge but also experience a variety of cultural activities that shape their understanding of the world. Through hands-on learning, music, dance, arts, and storytelling, we celebrate different cultures and traditions, encouraging creativity, empathy, and respect for diversity.

Our dedicated teachers design engaging activities that promote both cognitive development and emotional growth, making every day at our kindergarten an exciting journey of discovery!
